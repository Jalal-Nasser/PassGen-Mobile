PassGen Vault Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-09

PassGen Vault is a local-first password manager for iPhone. This policy explains what data may be processed when you use the mobile app.

Data used by the app

  • Email address, when you sign in with Apple or Google.
  • User ID, for account authentication and subscription-linked features.
  • Purchase and subscription status, through Apple and RevenueCat.
  • Encrypted vault backup data, if you enable cloud backup or sync features.
  • Camera access, only when you choose to scan a 2FA QR code.
  • Biometric authentication, only to unlock your vault on device.

Data not collected in plaintext

  • Your master password.
  • Your vault contents in plaintext.

How the app uses data

  • To authenticate your account.
  • To unlock subscription features.
  • To sync or restore encrypted backups when you enable cloud features.
  • To scan 2FA QR codes and generate authenticator codes locally.

Third-party services

Depending on the features you enable, PassGen Vault may use Apple Sign In, Google Sign-In, Supabase, RevenueCat, iCloud Drive, and Google Drive. These services process only the data needed to provide their respective functionality.

Tracking and advertising

PassGen Vault does not use personal data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.
